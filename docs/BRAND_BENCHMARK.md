# AZALI leather e-commerce benchmark notes

Reference brands used for direction:

1. Lost Dutchman Leather
   - Strong category-first navigation.
   - Reassurance messages such as warranty, handmade identity, and shipping notes.
   - Create-your-own/customization is visible as a serious commerce path.

2. Little King Goods
   - Clear collections and subcollections.
   - Strong handmade promise: goods are handcrafted from first cut and built to last.
   - Product families are easy to scan.

3. STR Handmade
   - Strong solo-artisan story.
   - Uses ready-to-ship status directly on product cards.
   - Makes contact feel human, especially for color/custom questions.

AZALI implementation choices:
- Cinematic craft-first home page.
- Categories before product grid to preserve premium browsing.
- Product pages with personalization options and lead-time transparency.
- Checkout as order request, not immediate confirmation, because handmade and custom goods need manual confirmation.
- Admin dashboard designed to replace a basic Excel tracker.
