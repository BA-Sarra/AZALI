# AZALI Photography Brief

The site is now designed to support premium leather photography. The placeholder SVGs should be replaced before launch.

## Hero

Shoot a full-bleed workshop scene or leather flatlay. Keep it dark, warm, atmospheric, and tactile. The site overlay will handle text contrast.

## Category Cards

Shoot one strong image per category:

- Wallets
- Notebooks
- Belts
- Bags
- Keychains
- Glasses pouches

Use landscape-oriented images, ideally around 5:3. Richly lit close-up leather shots on dark linen, raw wood, aged stone, or warm neutral surfaces will work best.

## Product Pages

Each product should have 3 to 4 images:

1. Primary image: front view, clean background.
2. Detail image: stitching, edge, clasp, closure, charm, or corner.
3. In-context image: on a desk, in hand, inside a bag, or near tools.
4. Texture image: close-up of grain and patina.

## Artist Page

Shoot hands working leather, tools on a table, offcuts, thread, edges, and the workshop atmosphere. This page should feel personal and human.

## Instagram Grid

Prepare 6 square images mixing product and process. These should eventually replace the placeholder grid before launch.
